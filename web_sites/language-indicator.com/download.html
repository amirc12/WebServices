  • Click the download button above.
  • Installer file will be saved on your computer.
  • When downloading is completed, run the installer file.
Once the installation is completed and Language Indicator is running, a new icon is added to the system tray:

Click on that icon to open Language Indicator options menu.
How to uninstall:
  • Press Win + I to open the Settings window.
  • Go to Apps > Apps & Features.
  • Scroll to find Language Indicator app or use the search bar.
  • Click the app, then select Uninstall and follow the prompts.
